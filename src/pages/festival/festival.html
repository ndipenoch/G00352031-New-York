Cherry Blossom Festival.

The Brooklyn Botanic Garden has the most lavish celebration of cherry blossoms in the city. This year, the festival takes place on Saturday, April 28 and Sunday, April 29 and includes Japanese cultural activities, music, and art. Kids enjoy sword-fighting play, so consider visiting the garden's famous Cherry Blossom Esplanade earlier in the season. Track the blossoms online to time your visit perfectly.
