Summertime Fun in Coney Island.

With its roller coaster rides and famous fairground foods, Coney Island is synonymous with summertime fun. the old-fashioned Amusement Park allows visitors to forget their cares and have a few hours of fun. Besides the Amusement Park and Beach Boardwalk, Coney Island also has museums, film screenings, and circus performances.

Coney Island, Brooklyn, NY, USA
